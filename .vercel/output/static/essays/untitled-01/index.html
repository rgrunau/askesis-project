Untitled: 01

September 22, 2025 · 1 min read · Read on Substack ↗

My goal, is to make this SubStack an open chronicle. Sometimes stories, some times thoughts and essays, and sometimes (when one pops up) poems. 

Please do not hold on too tightly when my days have ended. 
I will be with you as the wind moves through the leaves
In the rain seeped soil, soft under your feet.
And in the mote of dust floating on the air
In a blind cleaved ray of sun strewn across the floor.
